Your FREE 7-Day AI-Powered Meal Plan

A complete week of balanced, nutritious meals designed by AI to fuel your health goals.

Day 1 - Monday: Reset & Energize

Breakfast

Greek yogurt parfait with mixed berries, granola, and a drizzle of honey. Green tea or black coffee.

Lunch

Grilled chicken salad with spinach, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and lemon-olive oil dressing.

Snack

Apple slices with 2 tbsp almond butter.

Dinner

Baked salmon with roasted sweet potato and steamed broccoli. Season with garlic and herbs.

Day 2 - Tuesday: Plant Power

Breakfast

Overnight oats with chia seeds, banana, almond milk, and cinnamon.

Lunch

Lentil soup with whole grain bread. Side of mixed greens with olive oil.

Snack

Handful of walnuts and a small orange.

Dinner

Chickpea and vegetable stir-fry with brown rice. Season with turmeric and cumin.

Day 3 - Wednesday: Lean Protein Focus

Breakfast

3-egg omelette with spinach, mushrooms, and feta. Whole grain toast.

Lunch

Turkey and avocado wrap in whole wheat tortilla with lettuce and tomato.

Snack

Celery sticks with hummus.

Dinner

Grilled turkey breast with quinoa and roasted asparagus.

Day 4 - Thursday: Anti-Inflammatory Day

Breakfast

Golden milk smoothie: turmeric, ginger, banana, coconut milk, and a pinch of black pepper.

Lunch

Wild-caught tuna salad bowl with mixed greens, walnuts, blueberries, and balsamic vinaigrette.

Snack

Dark chocolate (70%+) with a handful of almonds.

Dinner

Baked cod with roasted tomatoes, olives, and capers over cauliflower rice.

Day 5 - Friday: Gut Health Focus

Breakfast

Kefir smoothie with mixed berries, spinach, and flaxseeds.

Lunch

Miso soup with tofu, seaweed, and edamame. Brown rice on the side.

Snack

Sliced cucumber and carrots with tzatziki dip.

Dinner

Sauerkraut and grilled chicken bowl with roasted vegetables and tahini dressing.

Day 6 - Saturday: Active Recovery

Breakfast

Protein pancakes made with oats, eggs, and banana. Top with fresh berries and maple syrup.

Lunch

Buddha bowl: roasted sweet potato, chickpeas, kale, avocado, and tahini dressing.

Snack

Rice cakes with almond butter and banana slices.

Dinner

Grass-fed beef stir-fry with colorful bell peppers, broccoli, and brown rice.

Day 7 - Sunday: Rest & Restore

Breakfast

Avocado toast on sourdough with poached eggs and smoked salmon. Herbal tea.

Lunch

Homemade vegetable soup with lentils and a side of whole grain bread.

Snack

Fresh fruit salad with mint and a squeeze of lime.

Dinner

Roasted chicken thighs with Mediterranean vegetables, olives, and feta. Side salad.
